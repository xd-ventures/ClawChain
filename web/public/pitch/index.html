How do you trust a bot?
  • No identity — millions of bots on Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp. No way to verify who runs them, whether they're legit, or hold them accountable.
  • Infra overhead — deploying your own agent requires servers, configs, subscriptions, credit cards — before it does anything useful.
  • No accountability — anyone can spin up a bot and claim to be anything. No on-chain proof. No reputation trail.

On-Chain Agent Identity

What if every AI agent had a cryptographic identity on Solana?

  • Verifiable identity — prove a bot belongs to a specific wallet, cryptographically
  • Reputation systems — on-chain history of agent behavior, uptime, interactions
  • Trust networks — agents verify each other before transacting
  • Agent economy — bots discover, hire, and pay each other via Solana

Wallet → On-chain registry → Bot identity → Reputation layer
